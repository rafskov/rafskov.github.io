SPIN Selling Outline

Welcome to my book outlines! Influence by Robert Cialdini

Chapter 1

  • People use shortcuts for thinking that are triggered by something specific - the word 'because', expensiveness as a proxy for quality where no other information or desire to learn exists
  • Shortcuts are accurate in the long run and save brain effort
  • Mimics exist in natural world to take advantage of shortcut thinking: dangerous female fireflies, and fake Amazon reviews
